VOICE CAST

Shizuku Tsukishima (月島 雫, Tsukishima Shizuku): A 14-year-old junior high school student who loves books.

Original Cast: Yōko Honna

Disney English Dub: Brittany Snow

Seiji Amasawa (天沢 聖司, Amasawa Seiji): An aspiring violin maker who attends the same school as Shizuku.

Original Cast: Issei Takahashi

Disney English Dub: David Gallagher

Asako Tsukishima (月島 朝子, Tsukishima Asako): Graduate student. Shizuku and Shiho's mother and Seiya's wife.

Original Cast: Shigeru Muroi

Disney English Dub: Jean Smart

Seiya Tsukishima (月島 靖也, Tsukishima Seiya): Librarian. Shizuku and Shiho's father and Asako's husband.

Original Cast: Takashi Tachibana

Disney English Dub: James Sikking

Baron Humbert von Gikkingen (フンベルト・フォン・ジッキンゲン男爵, Funberuto fon Jikkingen danshaku): An anthropomorphic cat statue from Germany belonging to Shirō Nishi.

Original Cast: Shigeru Tsuyuguchi

Disney English Dub: Cary Elwes

Shirō Nishi (西 司朗, Nishi Shirō): Owner of a local antique shop. Seiji's grandfather

Original Cast: Keiju Kobayashi

Disney English Dub: Harold Gould

Yūko Harada (原田 夕子, Harada Yūko): Shizuku's friend at her school.

Original Cast: Maiko Kayama

Disney English Dub:Ashley Tisdale

Ms. Kōsaka (高坂先生, Kōsaka-sensei): The yōgo (health room teacher) at Shizuku's school whom she and her classmates sometimes hang out with.

Original Cast: Minami Takayama

Disney English Dub: Vicki Davis

Kinuyo and Nao (絹代, ナオ): Shizuku's other school friends.

Original Cast: Mayumi Iizuka, Mai Chiba

Disney English Dub:Mika Boorem, Abigail Mavity

Sugimura (杉村): Yūko's crush and Shizuku's friend.

Original Cast: Yoshimi Nakajima

Disney English Dub: Martin Spanjers

Shiho Tsukishima (月島 汐, Tsukishima Shiho): Shizuku's older sister and a college student.

Original Cast: Yorie Yamashita

Disney English Dub: Courtney Thorne-Smith

Kita and Minami (北, 南): Nishi's musician friends.

Original Cast: Toshio Suzuki, Naohisa Inoue

Disney English Dub: (Kita) Walker Edmiston, (Minami) Corey Burton
